

# Hospitality Process Optimization – Guest Check-in Improvement

## Project Overview
This project focuses on optimizing the **guest check-in process** in a luxury hospitality environment. The objective was to reduce guest wait time, improve operational efficiency, and enhance overall guest experience.

This project was executed as part of independent consulting work under **Dreamfinity Consulting & Academy**.

---

##  Problem Statement
The existing check-in process was inefficient and time-consuming:
- Average check-in time: **12–15 minutes**
- Long queues during peak hours  
- Manual verification and data entry  
- Lack of real-time coordination with housekeeping  

---

##  AS-IS Process (Current State)

```text
[Guest Arrival]
      ↓
[Wait in Queue]
      ↓
[Manual Reservation Lookup]
      ↓
[Manual ID Verification]
      ↓
[Data Entry in PMS]
      ↓
[Room Availability Check with Housekeeping]
      ↓
[Room Allocation]
      ↓
[Payment Processing]
      ↓
[Key Card Issuance]
      ↓
[Guest Proceeds to Room]

Pain Points:
Long wait times
Manual processes and duplication
Delayed room readiness updates
Poor inter-department coordination

**TO-BE Process (Optimized State)**
[Pre-Arrival: Digital Check-in Form]
      ↓
[Auto Verification of Guest Details]
      ↓
[Pre-Assigned Room Allocation]
      ↓
[Real-Time Room Status Sync with Housekeeping]
      ↓
[Guest Arrival]
      ↓
[Express Check-in Counter]
      ↓
[Quick ID Confirmation]
      ↓
[Instant Key Card Issuance]
      ↓
[Guest Proceeds to Room]

**Improvements:**
Faster check-in process (<5 minutes)
Reduced manual effort
Real-time coordination
Improved guest experience

Process Efficiency	Low	Improved
